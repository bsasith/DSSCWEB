Mission is to

Develop skilled and competent sons of D.S. with a sense of understanding and compassion for others in an amicable multi ethnic atmosphere that fosters national and religious harmony, possessing a balanced personality-gaining total learning experience to become global citizens.

  • Sense of understanding
  • Compassion for others
  • Balanced personality
  • Total learning experience

DSSC’s shared vision is to create proud sons of D.S. to the society, nurtured with a passion for knowledge, competence and skills whilst enabling each to confront with global challenges. D.S. Senanayake College shines as the second-largest multi-ethnic school in Sri Lanka, welcoming students from diverse ethnic backgrounds. By fostering unity and harmony among its students, it champions inclusivity and celebrates cultural diversity. Together, let’s pave the way for a brighter future of Mother Sri Lanka.
